Who We Are

We are the Cockroach Janta Party. Not a political party. We are citizens — Gen Z, farmers, students, workers, mothers — everyone who has been ignored, silenced, or cheated. Like cockroaches, we survive everything. And we are done staying quiet.

"We pay taxes. We deserve better than a cockroach life."
What We Demand
  • Accountability — Every MLA, official, cop must answer publicly.
  • Infrastructure Justice — Roads that don't kill us. Clean water. Real schools. Real hospitals.
  • Police Reform — Zero bribery. No illegal arrests. Serve people, not the powerful.
  • Women's Safety — FIR filed same day. Real protection. No victim blaming.
  • Rural Equality — Your village deserves Mumbai-level development. It's your right.
  • End Corruption — From traffic bribe to MLA fund theft. Every incident documented.
  • Honest Media — Stop reporting nonsense. Cover corruption. Cover truth.
  • Secular India — We are one India. Religious conflict is a distraction from stolen taxes.
  • Consumer Rights — MRP is law. Discount offers are binding. Fight back.
  • Civic Sense — Start with the government itself.
"You are not powerless anymore."
What This Platform Does

Unlike Instagram where posts disappear — every report here is permanent. Visible until fixed. Every corrupt politician, broken promise, ignored complaint — documented forever. This is not a social media feed. This is India's civic memory.
